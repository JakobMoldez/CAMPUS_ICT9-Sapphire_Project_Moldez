Dont leave your kitchen unattended
 If you dont pay attention to your stove or anything that needs heat and you leave it like that for a while then a fire would break out, so to avoid this we must always check wheter he have turned everything off in our kitchen to prevent a fire.
Check your heating sources
 Some items need heat to operate, so we should try to keep them away from amything flammable and have a professional check them from time to time
Store flammable items correctly
 There are things that are flammable that we use in our everyday lives and its suggested that we keep these items in a cool area away from any heaters
Keep a fire extinguisher in your house
 Put some fire extinguishers around your house, anywhere approachable or near so that when a fire breaks out you can safely go get a fire extinguisher and extinguish the fire
